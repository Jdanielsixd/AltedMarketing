About Alted

Making Business Marketing Stronger

Alted aims to develop accessible, inclusive and seamless marketing content. While uploading a company's social media post, users will learn about ALT text, hashtags, and image descriptions. After being introduced to basic concepts and tutorials, users will be able to upload and create content that will inspire and motivate persons with a visual impairment. 

Our Company Mission
